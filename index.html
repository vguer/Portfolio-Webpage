Who Is She?

Vanessa is an artist from Guadalajara, calling the Pacific Northwest her home. At home, Vanessa gets lost behind pages and screens- reading, drawing, inspired by all the beautiful things. She enrolled in Epicodus to pursue the satisfaction in solving problems with creative solutions.
